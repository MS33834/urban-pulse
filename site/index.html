What is this

A long-running urban economic observatory. Auto-collects public data from 10 Chinese cities, runs forecasts, generates reports. Updates annually on January 15th — for as long as the internet exists.

Started as a graduation project. Built to last forever.

What it can do

🏙️ Competitiveness Index

Entropy-weighted scoring across 9 dimensions: capital, technology, institutions, industry chain, cost, scale, agglomeration, location, and healthcare. Rank cities, compare dimensions, generate per-city reports.

📈 Time-Series Forecasting

GDP, population, fiscal revenue projections with ARIMA/GARCH/Theta models. Confidence intervals included. Multi-city comparison supported.

🔍 Enterprise & Policy Analysis

Cost environment, supply chain maturity, and policy support scoring per city. Built for location decisions and investment research.

Data Sources

Public data, manually curated into JSON under data/cities/. Each city file stores annual records with 19 economic indicators:

  • GDP, GDP growth rate, fiscal revenue
  • Population, urbanization rate
  • R&D intensity, high-tech industry ratio
  • Land prices, salary levels, energy costs
  • Supplier counts, delivery times, tax incentives
  • And more — see the methodology docs

Roadmap

  • Expand to 15-20 cities
  • Auto-scrapers for education, environment, infrastructure data
  • Multi-city VAR forecasting (economic ripple effects)
  • Forecast accuracy tracking vs actuals
  • Jupyter notebooks for reproducible research

这是什么

一个长期维护的城市经济观测站。自动采集 10 座中国城市的公开数据，跑预测，出报表。每年 1 月 15 号自动更新一次，重新跑一遍全量预测。长期跑着，年复一年。

毕业设计。但毕业后不想扔在那吃灰。

能做什么

🏙️ 竞争力指数

基于熵权法的城市竞争力评价，覆盖资本力、科技力、制度力、产业链力、成本力、规模力、聚集力、区位力、生命健康力 9 个维度。排名、雷达图、热力图、单城市分析报告一键生成。

📈 时间序列预测

GDP、人口、财政收入等多指标预测（ARIMA/GARCH/Theta），含置信区间，支持多城市对比。

🔍 企业与政策分析

城市成本环境、供应链成熟度、政策支持力度评分。帮助企业做选址决策和投资研究。
